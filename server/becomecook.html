How it works
1
Open your kitchen
Let’s get you set up ready to sell your food!
Quick phone call
Let’s have a quick phone call so we can get to know you and answer any questions.
Kitchen visit
It’s important for us to come over to see your kitchen and taste your food. The visit takes about 30 minutes, and while we’re there we’ll help you setup your profile.
Photoshoot
Smile for the camera while we take high-quality photos of your food to start you off nicely.
Share your creations
During the kitchen visit, we’ll help you set up for first few items, giving them a name, description, price, etc.
2
Start getting orders
You’re live! Respond to customers and get ready to cook!
Receive requests
You’ll get a text notification when someone requests an order. You don’t have to cook until after you receive a request, that means no food goes to waste.
Accept or decline requests
You have 12 hours to accept a request, otherwise it will automatically be declined. We encourage you to work out the details with your customers and suggest times that work for both of you.
Message your customers
You will also be able to have a real conversation with your customers through the messaging feature. Just like Facebook messenger or any other chat app.
3
Start earning money!
The fun part! Make food for people. Make money for you.
Direct deposit
You will receive a direct deposit to your bank account within 48 hours of completing a request. Our SSL-encrypted payments platform keeps you and your customers secure.
Pickup or delivery
Either the customer or a 3rd party delivery service will come to your house at the scheduled time and pick up the food. You can also offer personal deliveries to get more orders and make extra money.
Build your brand
If you have ambitions of being a private cook, or starting a small food business, then Gigamunch is the perfect starting place to build a brand, or grow a base of loyal customers.
Get real feedback
Our rating and review system will help you test out crazy new ideas and see what people really think.
