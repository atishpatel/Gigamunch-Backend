Laura Rodriguez

I'm a self-taught chef who learned to cook in Italy and studied Asian cuisine when I lived in South Korea. I worked in restaurants in Chicago before I started my own catering company. I'm currently the Executive Chef Instructor of Meals 4 Health and Healing, a non profit that delivers free organic meals to cancer patients. I live on an organic micro farm where I raise my own chickens for eggs and meat, and all of the vegetables and fruit we need to sustain ourselves. In addition to that, I teach cooking classes to a cancer survivors' in Nashville. I'm sharing my food because I think everyone should have access to affordable healthful organic meals delivered right to their door!

Dulzida Cirak

I am a mother of two and have been cooking all my life. When I was young I lived in a very full house. We had big family dinners every night. I would cook with my mom and sisters. I moved to Tennessee 15 years ago, and I continue to cook dinners for my family every night. I am excited to see people actually love Bosnian food. I love sharing stories about my culture and tradition. I love cooking because community is very important in Bosnia, and I believe food brings family together.
