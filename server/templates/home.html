Travel the world from your dinner table

For those who want to discover the world,
we deliver ready-to-eat meals from a different country each week
so you can explore the world from your own dinner table.

Every Week, A New Journey

Meals Creafted by Local Immigrants

Every meal is designed by local immigrants to ensure the most authentic taste of their country. As you explore each region, you'll find a complete dinner with a dessert to leave you full and satisfied. No Cooking!

Listen to Cultural Music

Immerse yourself in the culture wiht a playlist of popular songs from that country. You can enjoy the music on Spotify or Youtube while you eat.

A Personal Culture Guide

Each journey comes with a personal guide. The culture guide tells a story about the immigrant chef and dives into their unique culture.

Experience cultures in a unique way

  1. Your journey will arrive at your doorstep before 5 pm in an insulated bag.

  2. Place bag in fridge when you get home. At dinner time, reheat -
    no cooking.

  3. Gather around the dinner table, and enjoy your journey to a new country!
